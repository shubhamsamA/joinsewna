The Explore Page is designed to help users easily discover and connect with independent fashion designers.
It blends clean minimalism with warmth — showcasing handcrafted products that reflect creativity and individuality.
The layout emphasizes clarity and trust through elegant visuals, ratings, and consistent branding.
Using SEWNA’s white-and-lime-green palette, it creates a boutique-like browsing experience that feels calm yet premium.
The goal was to make users feel inspired and confident as they explore designer work and find their perfect creative match.
